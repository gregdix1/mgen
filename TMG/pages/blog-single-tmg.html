Official "XO Tour Llife3" Lyric Video Released.

In case you missed it ,which you probably did, Lil Uzi Vert dropped the official lyric video for "XO Tour Llife" off his latest EP Luv is Rage 1.5. The new lyric video is laced with comicbook-like sequences which compliment the already released static video. "Xo Tour Llife"reached #7 on the Billboard Hot 100 so even if you don't bump his music and there are some of you out there, one can still appreciate the illustrations of the trippy vid.
